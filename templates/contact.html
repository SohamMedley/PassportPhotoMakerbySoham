Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PassportPro

How does the background removal work?

Our advanced AI technology automatically detects the person in the photo and removes the background. It then replaces it with a solid color that meets passport photo requirements.

Do I need to create an account to use PassportPro?

No, you can create up to 5 passport photos without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your photos and access them later.

Are the passport photos compliant with Indian passport requirements?

Yes, all photos created with PassportPro meet the official requirements for Indian passports, including dimensions, background color, and other specifications.

How long does it take to process a photo?

The processing typically takes just a few seconds. You'll be able to see and download your passport photo almost immediately after uploading.

Can I print the photos at home?

Yes, you can print the photos at home if you have a high-quality photo printer. Alternatively, you can take the digital file to any photo printing service.
