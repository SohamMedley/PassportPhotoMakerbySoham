About PassportPro

Creating perfect passport photos with advanced technology

Our Story

PassportPro was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make creating passport photos easy, affordable, and accessible to everyone.

After experiencing the frustration of getting passport photos taken at traditional photo studios, our founders decided to create a solution that leverages modern technology to simplify the process.

Today, PassportPro serves thousands of users across India, helping them create perfect passport photos that meet all official requirements without leaving their homes.

Our Technology

PassportPro uses advanced AI technology to automatically remove backgrounds from photos and replace them with appropriate colors that meet passport requirements.

Our system is designed to ensure that all photos meet the specific requirements for Indian passports, including proper lighting, background color, and dimensions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the passport photo process for everyone. We believe that getting a passport photo should be quick, easy, and affordable.

We're committed to continuous improvement and innovation to provide the best possible experience for our users.
